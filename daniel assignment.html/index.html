Nigerian Jollof Rice

A delicious West African one-pot dish made with tomatoes, peppers, and seasoned rice.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 6 people

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 5 large tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • Salt and seasoning cubes (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, bell peppers, and half the onions into a smooth sauce.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© the remaining onions.
  3. Add tomato paste and fry for 3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the blended mixture and cook until reduced.
  5. Add stock, curry, thyme, seasoning, and stir well.
  6. Pour in the washed rice and mix until well combined.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until rice absorbs all moisture.
